Greetings and Welcome!

Hi, name is Fernando Aguilar Ortega. I am a Neuroscience researcher in the Minnesota Inclusive Neuroscience Development Scholars (MINDS) program at the University of Minnesota. I recently graduated from St. Olaf College with a bachelors of arts double majoring in Psychology and Biology with a Neuroscience concentration. My research interest is to understand the neuroscience of neuropsychiatric disorders and to explore the complex brain circuitry. I am currently working on studying the anatomical connectivity of the posteromedial cortex in humans using non-human primates at the Heilbronner Lab. If you have questions about my research experience,or any questions, please feel free to reach out. I can be reached at: aguil194@umn.edu
