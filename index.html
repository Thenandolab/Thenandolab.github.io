Greetings and Welcome!

Hello, my name is Fernando, and I am a first-year Neuroscience graduate student at Baylor College of Medicine. I graduated from St. Olaf College with a Bachelor of Arts, double majoring in Psychology and Biology, along with a concentration in Neuroscience. I am deeply passionate about unraveling the neuroscience behind neuropsychiatric disorders and exploring the intricate brain circuits that drive complex behavior. Currently, I am conducting research in the Heilbronner Lab, focusing on the structural and anatomical connectivity of the posteromedial cortex in humans, using non-human primates as a translational model.
